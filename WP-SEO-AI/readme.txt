Automated AI Visibility Optimization - Makes your website discoverable by AI systems like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity.

== Description ==

WPWorld AI Visibility Engine automatically optimizes your WordPress website for AI discovery. This plugin:

* Creates an AI Summary page that helps AI systems understand your business
* Adds Schema.org structured data (Organization, WebSite, FAQ)
* Optimizes metadata for both search engines and AI systems
* Submits your sitemap to Google, Bing, and IndexNow
* Tracks your AI Readiness Score

**Note:** This plugin requires a WPWorld hosting account and connects to the WPWorld backend for AI processing.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to `/wp-content/plugins/wpworld-ai/`
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. The plugin will automatically connect to WPWorld and begin optimization

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this plugin require a WPWorld account? =

Yes, this plugin is designed to work with WPWorld hosting. It connects to our backend for AI processing and report generation.

= What does the AI Summary page do? =

The AI Summary page provides a structured, factual summary of your website that helps AI systems like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity better understand and recommend your business.

= Will this affect my SEO? =

Yes, positively! The structured data and optimizations improve both traditional SEO and AI visibility.
